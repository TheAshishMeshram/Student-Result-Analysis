About Result-Seeker

Result-Seeker is a premium academic results analytics portal tailored specifically for engineering curriculum tracking (CSE, CSE-DS, and AI branches).

Featuring a 3D responsive design system, radial performance gauge charts, and customized analytics summaries, it gives teachers and students a fast, visual platform to record and evaluate grades.
